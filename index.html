Idea , innovation, vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on fostering innovation, technology, and sustainable growth.

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on eco-friendly practices, and ensuring a greener future for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Improved infrastructure in Bangladesh 2.0 will focus on upgrading roads, bridges, and digital connectivity to enhance urban and rural development.

Empowerment

Bangladesh 2.0 strives for empowerment by fostering equal opportunities, and enhancing education and skill development for all.
